Types of space habitats

By building the first habitat with earth-based material and then building all subsequent habitats with space-based materials, we could build them at a much lower cost and build more of them in less time. What kind of habitats could we build?

Habitats

  • Planet Orbiting Habitat
    This type of habitat could be used for industrial processes like biomedical research and production or it could be used as a recreational facility that could include hotels, casinos, three dimensional sports and three dimensional performing arts. This habitat could be an earth observational and monitoring facility or it could be a combination of all of these functions.

  • Solar Exploration Habitat
    This type of habitat could explore our solar system in a completely different way with no time limit. The solar explorer, like other habitats, will be 100% self sustaining. Everything gets recycled; the facility is a closed loop biosphere. This self sustaining capability will allow us to travel to the furthest reaches of our solar system in safety.

  • Mining Habitat
    This type of habitat could safely explore Near Earth Objects or venture to the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter for minerals of all types to be used as building materials or go to the gas giants like Neptune for volatile gases that could be used as fuels.

  • Construction & Settlement Habitat
    This type of habitat could be used to carry settlement equipment and settlers to places like our moon, Mars or beyond. Once the habitat has arrived at its destination it will establish an orbit and then stage the deployment and construction of the settlement while providing a safe orbital facility throughout its duration.

  • Solar System Fueling Habitat
    Fuel extracted from space based sources will need to be distributed throughout the solar system to allow the establishment of trade routes for space commerce.

Platforms

  • Mining vehicle/platform
    Our solar system is extremely rich in natural resources from minerals and metals to gases. While the moon is an obvious first target, the asteroid belt and the gas giants represent the mother lode. The solar exploring habitat can be configured for long term exploration, mining, and processing. The finished product can be transported to a looping space ferry for transport back to earth for manufacturing.

  • Manufacturing platform
    The planet orbiting habitat can be configured as an orbiting manufacturing plant. Shipments from off world mining operations would be delivered for processing into finished manufactured goods.

  • Bio-medical research/manufacturing platform
    The main advantage of the planet orbiting habitat is its ability to create a variable gravity. Bio-medical research and manufacturing could use this benefit of the variable gravity in their processes; this will result in products that are not currently available.

  • Advanced research platform
    The planet orbiting habitat can be configured into any kind of advanced research platform where the research may be too dangerous to perform on earth or require special environmental conditions:
    • Astronomy
    • Energy Research
    • Hi-energy physics
    • Biological and genetic research
    The advantage of space based research is safety, terrestrial assets, both human and material, are kept safe.

Additional types

  • Orbiting Space Station — Planetary Environmental Monitoring/Research
    Earth's climate is dramatically changing today due to manmade and natural processes. These changes in our planets climate will have a profound effect on billions of people who live next to water as sea levels rise due to the melting of the polar caps and other bodies of ice worldwide. Monitoring and potentially manipulating our climate is of paramount importance and requires immediate action to avert trillions of dollars of losses and human suffering.

    One solution could be an orbiting station that acts as a base of operations that could produce specialized probes that are launched from an orbiting habitat into earth's orbit. These probes could be used for monitoring pollution, water migration, crop health, and many other specialized functions. Probes could be produced to deploy solar screens or disperse ozone or other chemicals in earth’s upper atmosphere to lessen or reverse the effects.

  • Rescue vehicle
    A smaller version of the solar exploring habitat can be produced and used as a rescue vehicle. One or more of them could remain in orbit near occupied areas to provide emergency response to ship repair, emergency evacuation, emergency rescue, medical response.

  • Continuously looping space ferry system
    Today's space transportation system uses a point to point or "Out and Back" approach when traveling to a destination like the moon. This practice requires a lot of energy for acceleration and breaking that once used is forever lost. The solar exploring habitat could be launched into a continuous elliptical orbit between any two objects. This will result in the conservation of the launch energy required, with only minor corrections required occasionally. This continuously looping space ferry system would be met by space taxis that would load and unload cargo and passengers at each destination.
