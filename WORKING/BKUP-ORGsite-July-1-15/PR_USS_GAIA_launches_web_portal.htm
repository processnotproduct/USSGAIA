USS GAIA launches web portal


WASHINGTON, DC, USA 18 APRIL 2011—United Space Structures has just released its much awaited web portal: www.USSGAIA.COM. United Space Structures is a development, design and construction enterprise focusing on creating habitable environments to support research, manufacturing, entertainment and commerce in space.

Bill Kemp, United Space Structures President and CEO, indicates, "If mankind wishes to live in space longer than a year or perhaps even permanently we will need to take gravity with us, we will need to create artificial gravity.  United Space Structures has filed with the US patent office an application for technology that can create variable gravity environments".

USS technology can create small, medium sized biospheres (as shown below) or considerably larger structures, all which support variable gravity environments.

This technology can also create very large solar photovoltaic collectors for energy generation at continuous 24 hours, 365 day a year operation.  That energy can be beamed down to any location on earth.

Potential applications for USS:

  • Orbiting Hotel/Casino/3D sports Arena.
  • Orbiting Space Station–Planetary Environmental Monitoring/Research/Remediation.
  • Orbiting solar energy array.
  • Manned space vehicle with the exploration range of the entire solar system

Space infrastructure applications

  • Rescue vehicle.
  • Continuously looping space ferry system.
  • Mining platform.
  • Manufacturing platform.
  • Bio-medical research/manufacturing platform.
  • Nanotechnology research/manufacturing platform.
  • Advanced research platform.
